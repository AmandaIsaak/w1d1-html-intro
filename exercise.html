I Built A Webpage

July 21, 2015

Amanda Isaak

I am so excited about building a webpage. I am looking forward to customizing the look of my website and creating unique features for my website visitors.

I don't think that this blog will have very much to do with cats or cute kittens, but since they seem to go viral on the internet, maybe that is a good strategy for gaining a loyal following...

I would rather write a blog about day-to-day life; my musings, adventures and favourite recipes! But I could include kitten (and puppy!) photos just to keep readers interested. Stay tuned for the cutest baby animals you ever did see, interspersed with simple life lessons.

Happy Reading!
